Project

BankMate

Product Design

BankMate is a customer-focused service designed to assist elderly and physically challenged individuals with banking needs. Employees visit customers' homes with a mobile ATM device to facilitate cash withdrawals via debit cards or UPI apps. The service ensures accessibility, convenience, and security, charging a minimal commission (1-2%) for transactions. The project includes modules for customer management, transaction tracking, and employee assignment, with data securely managed in a PostgreSQL database. Project Link.

Tourist management System

Product Design

The Tourist Management System is a platform designed to streamline the management of tourist-related services. It provides functionalities for managing tourist destinations, bookings, transportation, accommodations, and activities. The system allows tourists to view available packages, make bookings, and receive updates on their itineraries. Admins can oversee and manage tourist data, including bookings, payments, and service providers. It aims to enhance the user experience by simplifying travel planning and coordination while offering seamless management for service providers. Project Link.
